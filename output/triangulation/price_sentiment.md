# Price Tier vs. Sentiment

Based on 5457 HK reviews matched to a priced product (38 HK reviews excluded — no price on file for that product). Tiers are relative to the HK catalogue only; not comparable to Thailand pricing.

## Tier breakpoints (33rd / 66th percentile of HK product price)

- **Budget:** HK\$30 – HK\$168 (bottom third)
- **Mid:** HK\$168 – HK\$210 (middle third)
- **Premium:** HK\$210 – HK\$1120 (top third)

## Tier-level summary

| Tier | Products | Reviews | Avg. rating | % 1-star | % 5-star |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Budget | 340 | 3230 | 4.33 | 8.9% | 68.5% |
| Mid | 264 | 1162 | 4.35 | 9.6% | 72.8% |
| Premium | 301 | 1065 | 4.45 | 7.5% | 75.2% |

## Statistical test — overall (all brands combined)

**Overall — one-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.2111). No clear pattern found.
**Overall — five-star rate:** Premium-tier products show a higher five-star rate (75.2%) than Budget-tier (68.5%), consistent with higher-priced products more often meeting expectations (chi-square p=0.0).

## Statistical test — per brand

Only run for brands where every tier has at least 15 reviews; otherwise flagged as thin and skipped rather than tested on a misleadingly small base.

**Acuvue — one-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.3359). No clear pattern found.
**Acuvue — five-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.0651). No clear pattern found.

**Alcon — one-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.1225). No clear pattern found.
**Alcon — five-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.1563). No clear pattern found.

**Bausch & Lomb — one-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.2632). No clear pattern found.
**Bausch & Lomb — five-star rate:** Mid-tier products show a higher five-star rate (82.4%) than Budget-tier (68.2%), though the pattern doesn't map to a simple price-quality story (Mid-tier is the outlier) (chi-square p=0.0102).

**CooperVision — one-star rate:** no statistically meaningful difference across price tiers (chi-square p=0.2576). No clear pattern found.
**CooperVision — five-star rate:** Mid-tier products show a higher five-star rate (100.0%) than Budget-tier (67.4%), though the pattern doesn't map to a simple price-quality story (Mid-tier is the outlier) (chi-square p=0.0049).

**Olens — one-star rate:** not tested (missing tier(s): Mid, Premium).
**Olens — five-star rate:** not tested (missing tier(s): Mid, Premium).
